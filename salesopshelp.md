# Sales Operations Guide: How to Use the AI Sales Assistant

## Overview
This AI-powered sales assistant helps sales representatives handle customer interactions more effectively through real-time coaching, objection handling, and content creation. Here's how to use it during actual sales interactions.

## Step-by-Step Sales Interaction Workflow

### Phase 1: Pre-Call Preparation (Data Sources Tab)

**Before your sales call:**

1. **Upload Relevant Materials**
   - Navigate to the "Data Sources" tab
   - Upload product datasheets, case studies, pricing guides, and competitor comparisons
   - Upload customer-specific documents (previous communications, contracts, etc.)
   - The AI will analyze these documents to provide contextual assistance during your call

2. **Review Available Resources**
   - Check the file count to ensure all necessary materials are loaded
   - Use the search function to quickly locate specific documents
   - Verify all files uploaded successfully (file sizes shown for confirmation)

### Phase 2: During the Call (Objection Handling Tab)

**While on your sales call:**

1. **Activate Real-Time Coaching**
   - Switch to the "Objection Handling" tab
   - Click "Start Coaching" in the Real-Time Coaching panel
   - Keep your microphone active for live transcription and insights
   - Monitor the audio level indicator to ensure proper detection

2. **Use AI Chat Assistant**
   - Switch to the "AI Chat Assistant" sub-tab
   - Type quick questions like:
     - "Customer is concerned about pricing vs. competitor X"
     - "They're asking about integration with SAP"
     - "Need ROI calculation for 500-employee company"
   - Get instant responses based on your uploaded materials

3. **Handle Objections in Real-Time**
   - When customer raises objections, the AI provides:
     - Instant coaching insights (whispered in your ear if voice coaching enabled)
     - Success-rated responses from the knowledge base
     - Contextual suggestions based on customer profile

4. **Voice Coaching Features**
   - Switch to "Voice Coaching" sub-tab for conversational dashboard
   - Get real-time feedback on:
     - Conversation pacing
     - Customer sentiment analysis
     - Recommended talking points
     - Next best actions

### Phase 3: Post-Call Activities (Content Creation Tab)

**After your sales call:**

1. **Generate Follow-Up Content**
   - Navigate to "Content Creation" tab
   - The system automatically captures call notes and customer info
   - Generate personalized:
     - Follow-up emails
     - Proposal summaries
     - Quote documents
     - Meeting recaps

2. **Create Custom Content**
   - Use the content hub to create:
     - Tailored presentations
     - Custom case studies
     - ROI calculators
     - Implementation timelines

## Best Practices for Sales Reps

### During Customer Conversations

1. **Keep the Tool Open**: Have the application open on a second monitor or split screen
2. **Use Quick Queries**: Type short, specific questions for faster responses
3. **Monitor Insights**: Pay attention to real-time coaching badges and confidence scores
4. **Stay Natural**: Don't let the tool interrupt your conversation flow - use it as support

### Preparation Tips

1. **Upload Fresh Content**: Always upload the latest product information before important calls
2. **Customer Research**: Include any available customer background documents
3. **Competitive Intelligence**: Upload recent competitor comparisons and differentiators

### Follow-Up Optimization

1. **Capture Everything**: Let the system record call notes and customer details
2. **Personalize Content**: Use generated content as a starting point, then customize
3. **Track Success**: Note which responses and approaches worked best for future calls

## Troubleshooting Common Scenarios

### "The AI isn't understanding my industry"
- Upload more industry-specific documents and case studies
- Use the search function to find relevant uploaded materials
- Provide more context in your chat queries

### "Real-time coaching is too distracting"
- Switch to "whisper mode" for subtle audio cues
- Use text-only insights instead of voice coaching
- Practice with the test insight feature during non-critical calls

### "Need faster responses during calls"
- Pre-upload all materials before the call starts
- Use shorter, more specific queries
- Keep frequently needed information in the most recent uploads

## Success Metrics to Track

- **Response Time**: How quickly you can address customer objections
- **Objection Resolution**: Success rate of handling common objections
- **Follow-up Quality**: Customer engagement with generated content
- **Preparation Time**: Reduction in pre-call research time
